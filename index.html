BS view: CB ≈ Bond Floor (PV @ r+spread) + Conversion Ratio × Black-Scholes Call (European, no early conversion).
Binomial view: CRR tree with Tsiveriotis-Fernandes split — equity component discounted at r, bond component at r+spread, with American conversion at every node. Continuous-coupon approximation, 200 steps.
Issuer call (when enabled): hard call at call price from first call date — issuer caps value at max(call price, conversion value); investor responds optimally (force-convert if in-the-money). No soft-call trigger (e.g. "stock > 130% × N/M days") modeled.
Takeover makewhole follows the standard time-value/offer formulation (NY-law style). Real prospectus tables use pre-computed grids with linear interpolation.
Standard practitioner approximations — does not capture credit-equity coupling, embedded calls/puts, dilution, or path-dependent features.
